The Better Choice

Our Progear Flux Trainer is designed to deliver smooth, quiet and realistic road riding.
Our fluid system delivers exceptional road-like resistance, and becomes harder to push through the harder you pedal.

Designed to deliver smooth, quiet and realistic road riding.

  • 1. Fluid System - Filled with just the right amount of fluid to calibrate appropriate resistance.
  • 2. Impeller / Rotor - Connected to drive shaft which pushes the fluid around. Creates higher resistance per rpm.
  • 3. Heatsink Fins - Fins increase surface area to dissipate the heat created by pedalling.
  • 4. Weighted Flywheel - Appropriately weighted to generate momentum and simulates coasting

Aluminium Rollers

Our aluminum rollers are more consistent, quieter and requires less maintenance than gel rollers. Reduces noise and tyre wear by up to 50%.

Adjustable Roller

Our trainer can be adjusted to suit 700c, 26”, 27” wheel sizes.

Wide base and anti-slip feet

Our strong steel frame comes with a wide base and anti-slip feet for extra stability during hard workouts and pedalling.

Quick Release Lever

Designed to fit common road and mountain bike frames, and includes a steel quick release skewer to match our system.
Suitable for bicycles with an axle width between 150-210mm

Quick Release Skewer & Front Riser Block included

Front Wheel Floor Riser block to stabilise your front wheel.

Foldable

Easily foldable with a low profile to save space and easy storage. Sets up in seconds when unfolding.

Easy Assembly

Instruction manual and tools are provided for a quick 10 minute assembly.
